# ÉLAN Brand Context

ÉLAN is a women’s strength private club.

It should feel more like a private wellness / training atelier than a standard gym. The experience is calm, focused and refined. The brand should communicate privacy, discipline, elegance and serious training without aggressive fitness clichés.

The audience is women who want:
- a private and calm place to train
- structured strength training
- a premium environment
- less noise and crowding
- better guidance
- consistency and discipline
- a refined atmosphere

ÉLAN should feel:
- minimal
- architectural
- elegant
- quiet
- premium
- intentional

ÉLAN should not feel:
- loud
- commercial
- crowded
- cheap
- influencer-driven
- masculine-aggressive
- neon fitness
